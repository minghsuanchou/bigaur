Company Profile

More than 30 years of design and planning experience in the paper making industry. Biguar Machine Technology Wuxi Co., Ltd. is a professional machine manufacturing company, with high-quality equipment, expert measuring methods, and are dedicated to extensive training and professional development of our employees.

New factory funded in 2017, located in the Hu Tai Industrial Zone, Wuxi, Jiangsu in the Yangtze River Delta Economic Zone - the most developed area of China. We specialize in manufacturing paper making preparation equipment, paper pulp molding equipment, together with development and manufacture of molds.

Due to huge biodegradable product requirement, paper pulp molding market rapid growth in market demand. We setup this new facility to developing new paper molding process technology, manufacturing high efficient stock preparation equipment and high throughput forming machine.
Based upon customers’ needs and demands, we customized everything from the process flow and the design of the factory, the equipment design and production, to the installation and construction of machinery. The Biguar team has the ability to provide the best technology and service to help our customers perfect and successfully carry out their plans.
